About

Prof. Dr. I Gede Puja Astawa is a researcher and lecturer in the field of Telecommunications Engineering. His research focuses on Wireless Communication Systems, Software Defined Radio (SDR), OFDM systems, and digital signal processing.

Research Interests

  • Wireless Communication Systems
  • Software Defined Radio (SDR)
  • OFDM Communication Systems
  • Digital Signal Processing
  • 5G and Future Wireless Networks
